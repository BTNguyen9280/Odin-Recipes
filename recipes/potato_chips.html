Homestyle Potato Chips

Description

This recipe will show you a simple and easy way to make a delicious batch of homestyle potato chips!

Homemade potato chips are fun and easy. Guaranteed they won't last long! A food processor with a slicing attachment is very helpful for slicing the potatoes. Experiment with the thickness; you may like them thicker or thinner. I like to use olive oil for frying, but you can use safflower, corn, or peanut oil as well as vegetable oil.

Ingredients

  • 4 medium potatoes, peeled and sliced paper-thin
  • 3 tablespoons salt or favorite seasoning
  • 1 quart oil for deep frying

Steps

  1. Wash and slice potatoes paper-thin, transferring to a bowl of cold water as you slice them.
  2. Drain slices and rinse under cold water. Refill bowl with water, add 3 tablespoons of salt, and put slices back in bowl.
  3. Let the sliced potatoes soak in salty water for about 30 minutes.
  4. Drain and rinces slices again. Pat dry.
  5. Heat oil in a deep-fryer to 365 degrees F.
  6. Working in small batches, fry until golden.
  7. Season potato chips with your favorite seasoning as soon as they are removed from the oil.
  8. Serve and Enjoy!
